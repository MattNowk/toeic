⏳ Les Temps en Anglais (Super Facile !) 🐾

😸
Present Simple
Habitudes, vérités générales.
" The cat sleeps all day. "
😼
Present Continuous
Action en cours (BE + -ING).
" The cat is sleeping right now. "
🙀
Past Simple
Action terminée dans le passé (-ED ou irrégulier).
" I caught a mouse yesterday. "
😻
Present Perfect
Bilan présent d'une action passée (HAVE + Participe passé).
" I have already eaten my kibble. "
😽
Future (Will / Be going to)
Projets, prédictions.
" I will catch that laser pointer! "
